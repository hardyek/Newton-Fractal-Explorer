Newton's Fractal Explorer

Explore the Newton's Fractal for the polynomial z³ + 1 = 0

Move around the fractal using the controls in the top left.

Control the quality of the fractal with the 1-5 scale in the bottom left.

Whilst moving use a lower quality to make it faster to render.

Then when you get to a position you want to see in full detail improve the quality.

Rendering in quality 4 or 5 will take a few seconds please be patient.
